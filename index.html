It starts with a conversation about your goals, timeline, and what success looks like. From there I put together a clear scope and plan. Once we align, I design and build in stages, sharing progress as I go so feedback stays easy. The goal is always a smooth handoff with a site you actually know how to manage.

What does ongoing support look like?

I offer flexible ongoing support to keep your website performing at its best. Whether it\'s adding new features, optimizing performance, or updating content, I\'m available to help your site evolve. We can work out a monthly retainer or simply engage on an as-needed basis.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

Revisions are built directly into my process. I share progress at key milestones so you can provide feedback while it\'s easy to adjust. I prefer maintaining a tight feedback loop through email or WhatsApp, ensuring the final product perfectly aligns with your vision.
